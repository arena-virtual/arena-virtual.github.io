Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Arena Trampolim mobile app ("the app", "we") handles user information ("you") when using our services. By installing and using the app, you agree to the terms described in this document.

1. Collection of personal data

The Arena Trampolim app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. We do not request registration, login, name, email, phone number, address, precise location, or any other information that could personally identify you.

2. Permissions and sensors used

To function properly, the app accesses the following sensors on your device:

  • Accelerometer: used to detect movements, jumps and speed variations while using the minigames. Data is processed in real time on the device itself, exclusively to control the game.
  • Gyroscope: used to detect device orientation and tilt, allowing you to control the in-game character through body movement. Data is processed in real time on the device itself.

Sensor data is not recorded, stored, transmitted, or sent to any server. It exists only in memory during gameplay and is discarded at the end of use.

3. Data stored on the device

The app may store, locally on the user's own device, some game preferences (such as scores, selected character, and minigame progress). This data stays exclusively on your device, is not sent to any server, and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.

4. Third-party services

Currently, the app does not integrate with third-party services such as analytics tools, advertising, social login, or sharing. If this changes in future versions, this policy will be updated and users will be properly informed.

5. Children

The app is intended for all audiences. Since we do not collect any personal data, the app complies with children protection laws (such as COPPA and GDPR-K).

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated periodically. The date of the last revision will always be shown at the top of this document. We recommend that you check this page from time to time.

7. Contact

For questions, suggestions, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or the app, please contact us via email: arenatrampolim.contato@gmail.com.
