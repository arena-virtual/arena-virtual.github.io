About

The park got a layer of play.

Arena Trampolim is an indoor park with trampolines, slides and a foam pit. The official app uses your phone's sensors — accelerometer and gyroscope — to turn your favorite attractions into score-based minigames.

Every jump, every tilt, every move becomes input. No need to stare at the screen the whole time. No losing focus on the real fun.

Coming soon to Android and iOS, free and with no personal data collection.

How it works

No AR. No touch. Just movement.

01

Pick a minigame

Select the game in the app that matches the attraction you're on at the park.

02

Put the phone away

Pocket it or strap it to your arm. From here on, the app only reads sensors.

03

Move

Jump, slide, spin. The accelerometer and gyroscope do the rest. Your score builds up.

04

See the result

At the end of each round, check your score, your records, and climb the leaderboard.
